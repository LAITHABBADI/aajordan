What is Narcotics Anonymous (NA)?

Narcotics Anonymous is a nonprofit fellowship of recovering addicts who meet regularly to help each other stay clean from drugs. Our 12 steps of NA provide a structured path to recovery from addiction. We operate without medication, doctors, or professional counseling.

The NA program offers recovery through the twelve steps and group support. We welcome all addicts regardless of the specific drugs used. Online NA meetings are available when you can't attend in person. Many members find that working with a sponsor in NA helps strengthen their recovery journey. The only requirement for membership is a desire to stop using drugs.

Signs of Drug Addiction: Do You Need NA?

  • Do you experience withdrawal symptoms when trying to stop using drugs?
  • Have you tried detox or rehabilitation centers without lasting success?
  • Do you feel powerless over your addiction despite wanting to stop?
  • Has drug use damaged your relationships, work, or health?

If you answered yes, NA Jordan offers a solution. Our 12-step program has helped millions worldwide achieve lasting sobriety from drug addiction. Join our NA groups near me in Amman today.
